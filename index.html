The piece we want to talk about is the layer above the hour. You already carry it around in your head. The question is whether any of it lives outside your calendar in a form a client or a peer could pick up and use.

The 60 / 30 / 10 frame

We think about orchestration in a 60/30/10 frame. Sixty percent of most small-business digital work is traditional code and database plumbing. Thirty percent is rule-based logic: the playbook, the checklist, the SEO audit, the content brief. Ten percent is a genuine AI problem.

Teams who skip the distinction burn budget on the wrong layer. For a one-person studio, the 30 percent is the leverage layer. That is the packaged methodology that turns your hours into a product.
